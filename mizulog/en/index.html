1Member Tracking

The whole family,
in one app.

Add Dad, Mom, the kids, grandparents. Just a name and a photo — and each member gets their own log.

Swipe the tabs on the home screen to see everyone's progress at a glance.

2Auto Goal Calculation

"How much should
my child drink?"

Enter age and weight — the app calculates a daily target automatically, adjusts the baseline by age, and adds more on exercise days.

Want to set your own target? You can override with a fixed number anytime.

* For safety, we don't auto-calculate goals for infants under 1 year old.
3Hot Day Mode

The app tells you
when it's a hot day.

The app checks today's forecast high for your location. If it exceeds your threshold, the daily goal automatically bumps by +10%, and the app gently suggests "drink a bit more today."

We never access your location in the background. Temperature is fetched only once, at the start of the day.
4Gentle Reminders

Notifications timed
to your day.

Set your wake-up time and the reminder interval — the app schedules the day's nudges automatically. You can also define a quiet window so notifications stay silent while you sleep.

All of today's reminder times are visible in settings — no surprises.

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device.

No account, no email needed. Your family's hydration data stays with you.

  • All records stay on your device. Nothing goes to the cloud.
  • No sign-up. We never ask for an email address.
  • We never access location in the background.
  • The app works fully even if you decline ad tracking (IDFA).
